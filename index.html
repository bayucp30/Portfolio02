Tentang

One fine afternoon, we were playing an online chat app called omegle, which is now gone. We chat some random people, get acquainted, skip, and next, until we meet in the chat room. We got to know each other, chatted, and shared cell phone numbers. Fyi, one of us lives in Kalimantan and the other one lives in Malang, and it is 2 different islands. Long story short we continued our conversation by phone, continuously, until and without realizing it we were dating. Like other people when starting a relationship, we started casually and more relaxed, gradually the fights began to occur frequently as the feeling of love increased.

We have been together for a very long time since 2015, we have many stories that we want to share and tell anyone who reads this article. Our relationship was not easy, but not very difficult because we always believed that good days would always come after bad days. We had a long distance relationship for about 5 or 6 years, and during our long distance relationship we only met 3 times, it was very hard for us. Almost every day we fought because of the lack of trust in each other at that time. As the saying goes the key to a relationship is communication, and that's the main thing that keeps us from all of this.
